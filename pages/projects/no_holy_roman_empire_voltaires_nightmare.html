Great Claims is a mod that allows a Great Power to claim vast sums of land at a substantial cost, allowing for expansion at a reasonable and balanced price.

This mod is no longer actively in development, and has been archived so that you may use it should such a desire come about, though it will require fixing any issues, and/or rolling back your Europa Universalis IV version.
