Single Player Magic

Version 1.0.0   |   Last Updated: 2024-03-23   |   Supported EU4 Version: 1.37
Steam Workshop        Github        Patreon
Single Player Magic is a mod not intended for public use but available for such use; It adds an in-game cheat utility which allows you to do various things in either Singleplayer or Multiplayer games that can be useful in various situations such as fixing something you do not agree with while trying to empire build, or simply just for fun casual empire building MP games, it is entirely up to the end-user.
