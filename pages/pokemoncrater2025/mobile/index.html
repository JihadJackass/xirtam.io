Pokémon Shqipe Mobile

Sign up or log in to the mobile version of Pokémon Shqipe Battle Arena where you can catch, battle and trade all of your favourite Pokémon online. And the best part is that it's completely free!

Why play on mobile?

The mobile version of Pokémon Shqipe is specifically designed and streamlined for your smartphone or tablet. This makes load times and your general play experience on mobile devices much more pleasant.
There are also exclusive mobile features that you won't see on the PC browser site.

What's the differences between the two games, feature-wise?

Pokémon Shqipe's mobile mirror comes with extras that you will not see on the PC browser version. Some of these extras include limited events, Pokémon and well... It's on the go.

Does the mobile site have everything that the PC browser site has?

Currently - No. We're working on getting everything included but currently there are a few features not implemented. For more information on what's not yet added, please read our FAQ.
